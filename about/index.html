About us

A big heart for helping children & families in need

Kids Against Hunger Sioux Falls is a local 501(c)(3) nonprofit that brings volunteers together to pack nutritious meals for those facing hunger—both locally and around the world.

Volunteer-powered

As a 100% volunteer organization with no paid staff and no governmental support, we focus on one thing: making an impact on hunger through meal packing events and community generosity.

What we do

We host volunteer food pack events and use donations to fund ingredients, supplies, and distribution.

Events
Food pack events

Volunteers work side-by-side to pack meals that help feed children and families.

Donations
Community support

Donations from individuals, churches, civic clubs, and businesses help make every event possible.

Distribution
Hope delivered

Meals are delivered to people in need locally and around the world.

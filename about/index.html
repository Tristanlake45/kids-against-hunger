About Kids Against Hunger Sioux Falls

Kids Against Hunger Sioux Falls is a local 501(c)(3) nonprofit that brings volunteers together to pack nutritious meals for those facing hunger—both locally and around the world.

Volunteer Donate

Volunteer-powered

We are a 100% volunteer organization with no paid staff and no governmental support. Our focus is simple: make an impact on hunger through meal packing events and community generosity.

What we do

We host volunteer food pack events and use donations to fund ingredients, supplies, and distribution.

Food pack events

Volunteers work side-by-side to pack meals that help feed children and families. It’s hands-on, high-energy, and meaningful.

  • Great for groups, churches, clubs, and businesses
  • Events are designed to be simple and fun to join
  • Reserve Your Corporate “Team-Building” Event today
  • Every shift helps move meals out the door

Funded by community support

Donations from individuals, churches, civic clubs, and businesses help make every event possible and keep meals moving to where they’re needed.

  • Every cent is Mission Focused to feed more people in need
  • Funds ingredients and packing supplies
  • Supports logistics and distribution
  • Directly increases meals packed

Hope delivered

Packed meals are delivered to people in need locally and around the world—because hunger doesn’t stop at borders. Your time and support help deliver nourishment and hope.
